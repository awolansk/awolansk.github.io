I am a Lecturer in the Quantitative Methods Area of the Mitchell E. Daniels, Jr. School of Business at Purdue University. I completed my Ph.D. in Economics at the University of California San Diego, and graduated with a B.A. in Economics and a B.S. in Mathematics from Indiana University. My research interests are in experimental economics, behavioral economics, and decision theory.
